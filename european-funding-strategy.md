## European Non-Dilutive Funding Landscape

#### **EIC Accelerator (€10.1B allocation)**
The premier EU deep-tech funding instrument for breakthrough innovations.

**Relevance to Helistats:** Directly targets TRL 5-8 dual-use technologies with disruptive potential.

**Funding Structure:**
- **Grant:** Up to €2.5M (70% funding for R&D, 50% for demo)
- **Equity:** Up to €15M (optional blended finance)
- **Total per project:** €17.5M maximum

**Application Pathway:**
- **Short application:** 2 months to decision
- **Full application:** If shortlisted, 4 months to final decision
- **Success rate:** ~5% (competitive but achievable for breakthrough tech)

**Ideal Timeline:**
- **Year 1:** Submit after 1 kg demonstrator flight (TRL 5 achieved)
- **Year 2:** Deploy grant for 100 kg military prototype (TRL 6-7)
- **Year 3-4:** Scale to TRL 8 with equity component

**Strategic Positioning:**
```
Application Focus: "Hybrid Buoyant-Electric Aircraft for Persistent
Surveillance and Heavy Lift - Disruptive cost reduction in aviation logistics"

Key Innovation Claims:
- 83% operational cost reduction vs incumbent helicopters
- Fail-safe design (dual lift sources)
- Zero-emission capable (green hydrogen)
- Dual-use: defense surveillance + commercial logistics
```

**Competitive Advantages for Selection:**
- ✓ Proven components (automotive H2, military turbines)
- ✓ Clear path to commercialization (military → civil)
- ✓ Large addressable market (€25.5B global, €8B+ Europe)
- ✓ Climate impact (90% emissions reduction)
- ✓ Female-founded or diverse team (bonus points)

### 5. European Regional Development Fund (ERDF)

**Regional Innovation Grants:** €200B across EU regions

**Use Case:** Establish manufacturing facility in specific region

**Typical Grants:**
- **Eastern Europe:** 50-85% of investment costs
  - Poland: €5-20M for aerospace manufacturing
  - Czech Republic: €3-15M for advanced manufacturing
  - Estonia: €2-10M for dual-use tech

- **Southern Europe:** 40-70% of investment costs
  - Portugal: €5-25M for Industry 4.0
  - Spain: €10-50M for aerospace clusters
  - Greece: €3-15M for defense manufacturing

- **Cohesion Regions (lower development):**
  - Up to 85% co-financing
  - Priority for job creation in strategic sectors

**Strategy:** Locate manufacturing in cohesion region for maximum grants

**Example:** Poland
- Grant: €15M for 200-employee facility
- Tax holidays: 10 years
- R&D tax credits: 50% of R&D costs
- Skilled workforce from aerospace tradition
