## Concept Overview

The **helistat** is a hybrid aircraft combining:
- **80% buoyant lift** from helium-filled toroidal envelopes
- **20% dynamic lift** from four electric rotors
- **Quadcopter control** for precision maneuvering
- **Fail-safe design** with graceful degradation

### Key Innovation
Four toroidal (donut-shaped) envelopes arranged in quadcopter configuration, each with a rotor passing through its center. This unique geometry provides:
- Distributed buoyancy supporting each rotor
- Unobstructed rotor downwash through center hole
- Compact footprint compared to traditional airships
- Modular, scalable architecture
